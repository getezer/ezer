# Ezer V3.1 — Product Logic Manifest
*For Product Team & Leadership*

## The Fortress Philosophy

A health insurance policy has two distinct dimensions that must be evaluated separately.

**Structural Maturity** answers: *How evolved is this contract?*
It is a function of time. Waiting period clocks run from inception. The longer a policy has been active, the more of its SI becomes unconditionally claimable. Maturity is not about how much SI exists — it is about how much of that SI is free from legal restrictions today.

**Functional Strength** answers: *How much clean firepower does this policy have right now?*
It is a function of money. A policy with ₹60L of immediately claimable SI is strong even if a small enhancement is still in a waiting period. Strength is not about the age of the contract — it is about the proportion of total SI that is available without restriction today.

These two dimensions are displayed side by side. A user can have:
- A MATURE / WEAK policy — old contract, all clocks served, but open legal errors
- A MATURING / STRONG policy — base fortress fully active, enhancement cooking, high clean SI ratio
- A DEVELOPING / MODERATE policy — base block still in waiting, but partial coverage available

Conflating them produces misleading signals. Keeping them separate gives an honest picture.

## The 75% Rule

A policy where ≥75% of total SI is "clean" (no running waiting periods, fully accrued bonuses) is rated at least MODERATE in Strength, regardless of running clocks on enhancement blocks.

**Why 75% and not 100%?**
Because a ₹5L enhancement block still cooking does not make a ₹60L fortress weak. The insured can hospitalise today and draw on ₹60L without restriction. The ₹5L is upside that hasn't arrived yet — not a gap in current protection.

**What counts as Clean SI:**
- Base/original SI blocks where all waiting periods are waived
- Plus Benefit / Multiplier Benefit where `status == "fully_accrued"` only
- Secure Benefit (Optima Secure) — always clean, no waiting period applies

**What does NOT count as Clean SI:**
- Enhanced SI blocks with any running waiting period
- Partially accrued bonus benefits
- Any block where specific disease or PED waiting is still running

**The Action Override:**
If even one ACTION exists (draft letter unsent, schedule error uncorrected), Strength is always WEAK — regardless of Clean SI ratio. A policy with a phantom block in its schedule is legally unstable. ₹300L of clean SI does not fix a contract defect.

## The Senior Exception

Indian insurers informally decline to add certain riders (particularly Unlimited Restore Benefit) to policies where the insured is above approximately 60 years of age. This practice is not documented in any policy wording or underwriting guideline. It is communicated verbally and never put in writing.

Without an exception, the maturity engine would penalise elderly policyholders for riders they cannot obtain — rating a 10-year, fully-served policy as MATURING instead of MATURE simply because an insurer refuses to sell them a rider.

**The Solution:**
A human curator adds a `rider_ineligible_due_to_age` field to the user schedule (Tier 2) when a rider is informally unavailable. The engine reads this flag and excludes the rider from the MATURE status check.

**What this is not:**
- It is not an age threshold hardcoded in the engine
- It is not a product-level rule in Tier 1
- It is a case-by-case curatorial judgment, recorded in writing with the reason

**Current application:**
Gupta Prasad Satapathy (age 77) — Unlimited Restore and Protector Rider marked ineligible. Policy correctly rated MATURE despite both riders being absent.

## Final Strength-Maturity Matrix

| Strength | Maturity | What It Means |
|----------|----------|---------------|
| STRONG | MATURE | Ideal state. Full fortress, all clocks served, riders active. |
| STRONG | MATURING | High clean SI, base clean, enhancement cooking. No open actions. |
| MODERATE | MATURING | Clean SI ≥75% but HIGH warnings from cooking blocks. |
| MODERATE | DEVELOPING | Partial clean SI, base block still restricted. |
| WEAK | ANY | Open ACTION exists. Contract legally unstable. Fix first. |
